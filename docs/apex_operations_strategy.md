# Apex Wellness Studios: Operations Strategy & Intelligence Report

## Executive Summary

Apex Wellness Studios operates five locations across the DC-Maryland-Virginia metro area. This report provides a comprehensive operational assessment, procurement strategy, and performance framework to support scaling decisions and studio-level accountability.

The business faces a critical inflection point: two locations are thriving, one is declining, and two are in early growth stages. Without centralized visibility and standardized processes, the owner risks making expansion decisions based on incomplete information.

## Procurement & Inventory Strategy

### Current State Assessment

| Process Component | Current Method | Problem |
|-------------------|---------------|---------|
| Equipment ordering | Owner places orders ad hoc | No visibility, frequent stockouts |
| Supply tracking | Spreadsheet per location, not shared | No cross-studio visibility |
| Vendor management | 6 different vendors, no contracts | No pricing leverage |
| Mat/prop inventory | Manual count monthly | Inconsistent, often missed |
| Uniform/retail stock | Ordered when noticed as low | Reactive, not proactive |

### Recommended Procurement Framework

**Initiative 1: Centralized Inventory Tracker**  
- Google Sheet with one tab per studio
- Color coded: Green (sufficient), Yellow (reorder soon), Red (critical)
- Estimated impact: $400–$600/month in avoided emergency orders

**Initiative 2: Minimum Stock Policy**  
Core items always on hand per studio:
- Yoga mats: minimum 15 per studio
- Resistance bands: minimum 20 sets
- Blocks and straps: minimum 30 sets
- Retail apparel: minimum 5 units per size/style

**Initiative 3: Vendor Consolidation**  
- Consolidate from 6 vendors to 2–3 preferred vendors
- Negotiate volume pricing across all locations combined
- Estimated savings: 8–12% annually on supplies

**Initiative 4: Demand-Based Reordering**  
- Replace calendar-based bulk orders with threshold-based triggers
- When item drops below minimum, order is triggered regardless of timing
- Reduces both stockouts and overstock situations
