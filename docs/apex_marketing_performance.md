## Alexandria Deep Dive — Conversion Problem Analysis

Alexandria's 18.3% conversion rate against a 35% benchmark is the most significant business risk in the portfolio. Additional ad spend will not fix this. The problem is post-arrival, not pre-arrival.

**Hypotheses to investigate:**
1. Front desk experience — are trial visitors being greeted, guided, and followed up with?
2. Class schedule — are trial-friendly beginner classes offered at convenient times?
3. Instructor quality — are trial classes taught by the strongest instructors?
4. Follow-up — is there a systematic 24-hour follow-up after a trial visit?
5. Pricing presentation — are membership options being presented clearly and at the right moment?

**Recommended immediate actions:**
- Survey last 30 non-converting trial visitors (email or text)
- Mystery shop the Alexandria trial experience
- Audit the class schedule for beginner-friendly options at peak hours (6–8am, 12–1pm, 6–8pm)
- Implement 24-hour follow-up sequence for all trial visitors
